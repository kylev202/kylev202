### Featured Project

#### 🌳 [Arbora](https://github.com/kylev202/Arbora)
A **local-first study assistant** that turns your documents and lectures into grounded, cited notes, flashcards, and quizzes — then schedules review with FSRS spaced repetition. Everything stays on your device.

- Ingests PDFs, slides, and audio/video lectures
- AI generates **only** from your sources, with citations on every item
- Review-before-trust: you approve material before it enters your decks

**Stack:** `React` · `Tauri 2 / Rust` · `Python (FastAPI)` · `SQLite + FAISS` · `Ollama`
